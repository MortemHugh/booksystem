About Us

Discover a world of comfort and elegance in the heart of ARUGA. Our thoughtfully designed rooms and suites offer a variety of options to cater to your specific needs and preferences. Each space is a sanctuary where modern luxury and timeless style converge, ensuring a memorable stay for every guest.

Who We Are

At ARUGA, we believe that every journey should be an extraordinary one. Nestled in the heart of Manila, our hotel is a testament to the art of hospitality, where comfort, luxury, and exceptional service intertwine to create a haven for discerning travelers.

ARUGA draws its inspiration from a rich legacy of providing unparalleled hospitality. With years of experience in the industry, we have mastered the art of anticipating and exceeding our guests' needs, making us the preferred choice for travelers from around the world.

Step into ARUGA, and you'll immediately sense the tranquility that envelops you. Our meticulously designed spaces provide a sanctuary from the hustle and bustle of the city, where you can unwind and recharge in utmost serenity.

Our rooms and suites are more than just places to stay; they are expressions of modern luxury and timeless style. Each one is a harmonious blend of aesthetics and functionality, ensuring that your stay with us is both visually pleasing and supremely comfortable.

Satisfy your palate at our world-class restaurant, where culinary artistry meets the freshest local ingredients. Whether it's a sumptuous breakfast, a quick business lunch, or a romantic dinner, our culinary team is dedicated to tantalizing your taste buds.

What truly sets ARUGA apart is our commitment to personalized service. Our attentive staff anticipates your needs and goes the extra mile to ensure your stay is nothing short of exceptional. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.

If you're hosting a conference, seminar, or special event, ARUGA offers state-of-the-art facilities and event planning services to make your occasion truly memorable.

Our wellness center invites you to rejuvenate your body and soul. Take a dip in our exquisite pool, work up a sweat in our fitness center, or indulge in a soothing spa treatment.

Beyond our doors, Manila awaits your discovery. ARUGA's prime location ensures you're just moments away from the city's cultural gems, shopping districts, and vibrant nightlife.
